Why Self Love is Important

For our website we decide focus on issues that surrounds us in society today. The main purpose of our website is to simply encourage self-love and self- care for young males and females. Self-love and self-care is important because it helps shapes who we truly are as a person. Young men and women toady struggle with finding and loving themselves due to the negative impact society has on them. The aim of our site is to provide young teens with helpful information, articles, tips, courses and recommended products which will enhance and improve their self-empowerment, love, care, and confidence.

One aspect of our website is geared towards young females. In the proper environment girls can grow up powerful and feeling secure in themselves. They can learn to take action, make positive choices about their own lives and do positive things for others. Although they grow up to be powerful and strong, like all us of, they may have times of insecurity and self-doubt. We focus on overturning gender stereotypes and inspire young girls to grow up happy, healthy, self-assured, and educated. Our website will be used as a platform on the world wide web to reach out to young females across the globe and help inspire, empower, and encourage them.

Our website will be designed to build young males and females up by encouraging them to love and appreciate themselves. Our goal is geared towards doing something positive and helpful in the hope that you males and females may benefit from our knowledge and what we have learned about dealing with self-care, empowerment, love, and confidence.
